# Food Setting

>Introduction

![Food](_images/food/food_1.png) 

In the `Hotel Service`, Administrators configure food type and menu items for the in-room IPTV ordering system, and can view, accept, complete, or cancel guest orders in real time. Guests place orders directly from their TV, and administrators manage the entire order workflow through the platform.

## Food Type

>Introduction

![Food Type](_images/food/food_2.png) 

In the `Food Type`, the administrator creates, modifies and deletes item categories.

Press `Add` button to create the new item category

![Food Type](_images/food/food_3.png)

<font color="red">**ID**</font>: In `ID`, The IPTV system automatically creates an ID number, which is used to sort the different categories, the smaller the number, the more advanced the display is

<font color="red">**Name**</font>: The `Name` is used to display the food of the category.

## Food List

>Introduction

![Food List](_images/food/food_4.png)

On the `Food List` page, the administrator manages the food items available for in-room ordering by creating, editing, or deleting products as needed.

Press `Add` button to create the new item for online shopping.

![Food List](_images/food/food_5.png)

<font color="red">**Image**</font>: In `Image`, Upload product images in Image and the images are displayed on the terminal, currently only one image upload is supported.

<font color="red">**Backgroud picture**</font>: The background image that will be displayed on the terminal when the user selects or clicks on the food item.

<font color="red">**Name**</font>: `Name` is used to identify the name of the f displayed in the terminal.

<font color="red">**Price**</font>: In `Price`, it is used to display the unit price at which the food is sold.

<font color="red">**Food Type**</font>: Select the `Food Type` for the food, and the food will appear in the corresponding category list on the terminal.

<font color="red">**Description**</font>: In `Description` is used to describe specific information and details about this food.

## Order Status

>Introduction

![Order Status](_images/food/food_6.png ':size=40%') 

In this page the administrator can view the details of the item order sent by the subscriber from the terminal. When the administrator receives the subscriber's order, he or she can `receive` the order by using the RECEIVE button or `cancel` the subscriber's order by using the DELETE button. After accepting the subscriber's order, when the order is completed, the administrator can click Finish button again to `complete` the whole order process.
